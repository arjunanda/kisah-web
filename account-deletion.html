Account Deletion

Instructions on how to delete your account and data.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please follow these steps directly within the Kisah application:

  1. Login to the Kisah application.
  2. Go to the Profile menu.
  3. Navigate to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account / Hapus Akun.
  5. Confirm the action on the confirmation screen.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

When you confirm account deletion, the following actions occur immediately:

  • Permanent Removal: All your account data is permanently deleted from our backend systems.
  • Irreversible: This action cannot be undone. Deleted data cannot be recovered.
  • Immediate Effect: There is no waiting period. Deletion happens instantly.
  • No "Soft Delete": We do not retain archiving or backup of your personal data after deletion.

After Deletion

Once the button is pressed and processed:

  • You will be logged out immediately.
  • Your session will be invalidated.
  • No confirmation email will be sent.
  • You will be redirected to the login or landing page.
