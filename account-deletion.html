Account Deletion Request

For Kisah App Users

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data for the Kisah mobile application. We respect your privacy and provide a simple, in-app mechanism to permanently remove your data.

1 How to Delete Your Account

To delete your Kisah account, please follow these steps directly within the application:

  1. Open the Kisah App and Login.
  2. Go to the Profile menu (bottom navigation).
  3. Tap on the Settings icon (gear icon).
  4. Select Delete Account / Hapus Akun.
  5. Read the warning and confirm the action by tapping Delete Permanently.

Types of Data Deleted

By performing the account deletion, the following data types associated with your account will be permanently removed:

Personal Information Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Profile Picture, and Login Credentials.
App Activity & History Watch History, Liked Episodes, Favorites/Watchlist, Search History, and Playback Progress.
User Generated Content Comments, Reviews, and any feedback submitted through the app.
Device IDs Associations between your user ID and device identifiers (FCM tokens, etc.) are severed.

Data Retention Policy

We practice a zero-retention policy for deleted accounts.

  • We do not implement a "soft delete" or "cooldown period". Once you confirm deletion, your personal data is erased from our active databases immediately.
  • We do not retain your data for marketing or analytics purposes after deletion.
  • Exceptions: We may retain non-identifiable, anonymized transaction logs strictly for tax and accounting compliance requirements (if you have made purchases), but this data is fully dissociated from your personal identity.

Impact of Deletion

  • Your access to purchased content (if any) will be revoked permanently.
  • You will be automatically logged out of the application.
  • This action is irreversible. You cannot recover your account or data later.
